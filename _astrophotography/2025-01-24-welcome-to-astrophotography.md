Welcome to the astrophotography section of my blog! This is where I'll share my adventures capturing the night sky.

## Getting Started

This is an example post to show you how the astrophotography collection works. To add new posts to this collection, simply create a new markdown file in the `_astrophotography/` directory with the following format:

```markdown
---
title: "Your Post Title"
date: 2025-01-24
---

Your content here...
```

> The dark theme you're seeing was carefully chosen to evoke the feeling of stargazing under a clear night sky. The deep blue tones mirror the twilight hour when the first stars begin to appear.

## What to Expect

Future posts will include:
- Deep sky objects I've captured (nebulae, galaxies, clusters)
- Equipment reviews and setups
- Processing techniques in PixInsight and other tools
- Dark site recommendations around the Pacific Northwest
- Lessons learned from the field
- Tips for beginners getting started

## The Joy of Astrophotography

There's something magical about pointing a camera at the cosmos and revealing details invisible to the naked eye. Whether it's the swirling arms of a distant galaxy or the delicate wisps of a nebula, each image tells a story billions of years in the making.

Stay tuned for more content about capturing the cosmos!
